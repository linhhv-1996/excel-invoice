FAQ

Do you upload my data to a server?

By default everything runs in your browser. You can opt‑in to sync configs to the cloud for multi‑device use.

Which formats are supported?

.xlsx and .csv. Export single PDFs or a ZIP of many files.

Can I add my logo and brand colors?

Yes. Upload PNG/SVG logo, set a primary color, and choose fonts.

Does it support VAT/GST and multiple currencies?

Absolutely. Configure tax rates, currency symbols, and locale formatting.
